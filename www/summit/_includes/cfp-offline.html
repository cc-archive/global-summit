Call for Participation and Proposals

Proposals for talks, workshops, hackathons, panels, presentations, performances, showcases and other activities are welcome.

Submit your proposal

Proposals that involve collaboration with local communities, public offices, or businesses and organizations not directly part of the Creative Commons community are welcome and encouraged.

Those who submit proposals will be given priority for scholarships to attend the Summit, although scholarships cannot be guaranteed.

A selection committee made up of Creative Commons community representatives from around the world will review all proposals and select sessions for the Summit.

Key Dates:

  • June 18 — call for participation/proposals announced
  • July 7 — offline-proposal submission deadline
  • August — session proposers notified of whether proposal is accepted or declined
  • End of August — Summit program is announced
  • October 14 -17 — Global Summit

Submit your proposal now and join us in celebrating, working on, and building the future of the Commons.
